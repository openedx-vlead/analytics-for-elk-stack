* Introduction
  This document will illustrate the requirements to design the analytics
  database service for Virtual Labs

* Requirements
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: requirements
  :END: 
** Req1
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: req1
  :END: 
  - Ability to store/retrieve the feedback and usage analytics of Virtual Labs
    running on =AWS= cloud platform in the form of =json= records.

** Req2
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: req2
  :END: 
  - Ability to store/retrieve the feedback and usage analytics of Virtual Labs
    running on =College Cloud= platform in the form of =json= records.

** Req3
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: req3
  :END: 
  - Ability to store/retrieve the analytics of users registered on =Virtual
    Labs= and =Outreach Portal= platforms in the form of =json= records.
